Adding Audio from Asset Library
Step 1: Access Dashboard
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Step 2: Select Projects Tab
Select the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Step 3: Navigate to Scenes
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
Step 4: Create or Open Scene
Create a new scene or open an existing one.
Step 5: Access Assets
Click on the Assets button in the navigation bar.
Step 6: Choose Audio from Asset Library
Select the Audio tab in the Asset Library and choose the desired audio clip from the list of audio files.
Step 7: Add Audio to Project
Click on the Create Audio button to add the selected audio to your project.
