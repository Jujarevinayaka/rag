Adding a Scene Template
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Select the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
Create a new scene or open an existing scene.
Click on the Assets button in the navigation bar to open the Assets panel.
Select Scene from the Asset Library categories.
Choose Anime Bedroom or any other template from the list of scenes.
Click on the Load Scene button to load the selected scene.
