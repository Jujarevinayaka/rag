Connecting Sketchfab Account
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Select the Projects tab in the User Dashboard.
Click on the Scenes button.
Create a new scene or use an existing scene.
Click on the Assets button in the navigation bar to open the Assets panel.
Switch to the third-party tab in the Asset Library.
Click on the Sketchfab icon to connect your Sketchfab account.
In the pop-up, click Accept to authorize True as Application to use your Sketchfab account.
Your Sketchfab account is successfully connected.
