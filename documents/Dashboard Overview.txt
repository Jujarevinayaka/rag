Dashboard Overview
How to Navigate and Manage Projects in the Dashboard
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar to access the dashboard.
In the Home section, you can find all the templates of the 3D editor. Click on a template to get started.
Switch to the Application tab to choose application templates and begin your project.
Go to the Projects tab in the navigation bar to view your projects. You can create a new storefront or open an existing virtual storefront.
Click on the Scenes tab in the navigation bar to access editor projects. You can create a new editor project or open an existing scene.
Managing Account Settings and Logging Out
Click on the Account Settings to modify your account details.
Click on Back to Dashboard to return to the dashboard.
Finally, click on the Logout button in the navigation bar to exit your account.
