Downloading the Project as GLB
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Click on the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Select the Scenes option from the drop-down menu.
Create a new scene or open an existing scene.
Click on the Share button in the top navigation bar to share your project.
Click on the Sync Now button in the sharing model to synchronize your data.
Select the 3D Model option in the sharing module to prepare your creation for download.
You can choose to separate child meshes.
Select the required format from the drop-down.
Click the Download button in the model to download the file in the selected format.
