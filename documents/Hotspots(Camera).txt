Hotspots(Camera)
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Select the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
Open the project or create a new scene.
Click on the Add button in the Editor's toolbar to add new assets or elements to your scene.
Select the Camera option from the Objects list in the sidebar.
Toggle the Preview switch in the Camera Settings panel to view the preview.
Adjust the field of view slider in the Camera Settings panel to the desired value.
You can lock the focus of the camera to an object by selecting the object from the Focus Target drop-down.
