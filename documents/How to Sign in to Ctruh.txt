How to Sign in to Ctruh
You have two options to log in:
Using your email ID and password
Using social logins
Using Email ID and Password:
If you choose to sign in using your email ID and password, follow these steps:
Enter your email ID in the designated field
Input your password
Click on the 'Sign In' button
Using Social Logins:
If you prefer to sign in using social logins, such as Google, follow these steps:
Select the account associated with the email you wish to use for sign-in
Proceed with Google, Facebook, Github, and LinkedIn sign-in
By following these steps, you will be able to successfully sign in to True. Enjoy your experience!
