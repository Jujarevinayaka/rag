Undo & Redo
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Select the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
Create a new scene or open an existing scene.
Adding a sphere to showcase undo and redo functionality.
Click on the Shape button in the toolbar to open the Shape Selection menu.
Select Sphere from the Shape Selection menu.
Click on the Surface button in the 3D Object panel to switch to Surface Editing mode.
Click on the Color Picker in the 3D Object panel to change the color of the selected object.
Select a new color from the Color Palette that appears in the 3D Object panel.
You can undo the color by clicking on the Undo button.
Click on the Redo button to reapply the color.
