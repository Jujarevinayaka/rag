Customising Ctruh Editor
1. Accessing the Dashboard
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
2. Navigating to Projects
Select the Projects tab at the top of the page.
3. Managing Scenes
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
Create a new scene or open an existing scene.
4. Customizing Scene Preferences
Click on Scene Preference to access customization options.
Adjust the background color by clicking on Background Color.
Change the grid color by clicking on the grid.
Modify the ambient light intensity using the slider in the Scene Preference panel.
5. Adding Background Audio
Click on the folder icon to select a background audio for the scene.
Select the desired audio file, such as Athena, from the Sound Library.
Play, pause, or reset the background audio using the options in the Scene Preference panel.
6. Audio Settings
Utilize the Loop option to loop the background audio.
Adjust the volume slider to control the audio volume in the Scene Preference panel.
By following these steps, you can effectively customize True Editor to suit your editing needs and preferences.
