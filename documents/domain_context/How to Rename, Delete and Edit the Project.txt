How to Rename, Delete and Edit the Project
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Click on the Projects tab.
Click on the Scenes tab to switch the view.
Click on the Rename option for a project in the context menu to rename the scene.
Click on Delete Project from the Project Options menu to delete the scene.
Click on Confirm to delete the project.
Click on Open in Editor from the Project Options menu to edit the project.
