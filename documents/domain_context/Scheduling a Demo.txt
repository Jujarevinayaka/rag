Scheduling a Demo
Click on the Book a Demo button in the main content area.
Click on the Book a Call button.
Select the date for the demo call by clicking on the date in the calendar based on your availability.
Pick a comfortable time.
Click on Next.
Add your details such as name, email, and a brief agenda for the meeting.
Enter your phone number to receive text messages.
Click the Schedule Event button to proceed with scheduling the event.
