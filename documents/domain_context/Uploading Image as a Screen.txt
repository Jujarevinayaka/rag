Uploading Image as a Screen
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Select the Projects tab in the navigation bar.
Click on the Scenes button in the top right corner.
You can create a new scene or open an existing scene.
Click on the Upload button in the navigation bar to add assets to your scene.
Select Image as Screen option from the Upload menu.
Toggle on the Always face the viewer option in the Properties panel for the screen to face you.
You can change the orientation to portrait or landscape based on your preference.
You can change the aspect ratio of the screen. We support 16:9, 1:1, 3:4, 1:2 for both portrait and landscape orientations.
