Uploading a 360 degree Background Image
Step 1: Accessing the Dashboard
Click on the Dashboard button in the navigation bar.
Step 2: Navigating to Projects
Select Projects from the navigation menu.
Step 3: Viewing Recent Projects
Click on the See All button in the top right corner of the Recent Projects section.
Step 4: Creating a New Project
Click on the Create Scene button to start a new project.
Step 5: Uploading a 360° Background Image
In the navigation bar, click on the Upload button to add new assets to your scene.
In the Upload modal, select 360° Background to upload a panoramic background image.
Click on the Replace button in the popup to overwrite the existing file.
